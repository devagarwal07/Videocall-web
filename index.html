Frequently Asked Questions

Is ConnectNow free to use?

Yes, ConnectNow offers a free plan with basic features. Premium plans are available for advanced functionalities.

How many participants can join a meeting?

Up to 100 participants can join a meeting on the free plan. Higher limits are available with premium plans.

Is my video call data secure?

Yes, all video calls are end-to-end encrypted to ensure your privacy and security.

Can I record my meetings?

Recording is available on our premium plans. You can save recordings locally or to the cloud.
